Privacy

This privacy page explains the limited data posture of Ergo Mouse Notes. The resource is published as static HTML, CSS, and image documents.

The site does not include user accounts, comments, checkout, lead forms, newsletter signup, product registration, document uploads, or private messaging.

The pages are not designed to collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, payment details, order numbers, office floor plans, staff schedules, private office photos, or installation documents.

Routine hosting systems may process basic technical information such as IP address, browser type, requested document path, timestamp, and referring URL for delivery and abuse prevention.

External links may lead to retailers, manufacturers, installers, or reference sites with their own privacy practices. Review those policies before sharing personal or business information.

Privacy is separate from About and Contact. About explains editorial purpose, while Contact explains safe correction boundaries.

Use the site for general planning, verify product details at the source, and keep sensitive office information in private business channels rather than public web pages.
